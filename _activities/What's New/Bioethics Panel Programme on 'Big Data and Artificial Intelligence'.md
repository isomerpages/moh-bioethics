<p>As part of the launch, the BAC and NLB organised a Bioethics Panel Programme
on 29 October 2022 at the Possibility Room, level 5, National Library Building,
with Emeritus Professor Lee Eng Hin, BAC Chair and Emeritus Professor,
Department of Orthopaedic Surgery, National University of Singapore (NUS)
and Emeritus Consultant, Division of Paediatric Orthopaedics, National
University Hospital (NUH), Guest-of-Honour.</p>

<p>The programme featured:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Introductory talk on bioethics (by Dr Voo Teck Chuan, BAC member and Assistant
Professor, NUS Centre for Biomedical Ethics)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Talk on the ethical dilemma of Big Data and AI use in Human Biomedical
Research (by Professor Julian Savulescu, Director, NUS Centre for Biomedical
Ethics)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Panel discussion on emerging ethical issues arising from the use of Big
Data and AI in biomedical research</p>
